What Are We About?

In an era where climate change poses a critical challenge to our planet, our initiative is dedicated to addressing one of its primary causes: carbon emissions. We understand that the path to a sustainable future is paved with informed choices, innovative thinking, and collective action.

FAQs

Why did we choose this cause?

We chose this cause to advance people's knowledge about carbon emissions as a whole, in particular, to provide an easy search tool about university websites' carbon emissions.

Why is this important?

This website is important because it is the first step to recognizing how important internet usage is against the fight for climate change.

Will Carbon Emissions Improve?

We sure hope so! More users learning about carbon emissions will increase awareness about the university website's usage!

What Can We Do To Help?

Enlist your name in our contact us sheet and automatically become our partner in the fight against high carbon emissions!
